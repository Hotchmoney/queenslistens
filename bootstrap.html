What's on your mind?

Queen's Listens is a peer-to-peer anonymous support service run by Queen's students, for Queen's students. Let us know whatever you're feeling, and one of our student volunteers will respond with feedback and encouragement.

Before you share, please note:

  • We are not trained counselors or medical professionals.
  • This service is not for handling crises or emergencies.
  • We do not guarantee our advice will work for you.
  • We are simply a group of students providing a place for you to vent your feelings.

If you feel like you need professional help, please call Good2Talk at 1-866-925-5454.

If you feel you are currently a threat to yourself or others, please call 9-1-1 or the Suicide Prevention Lifeline at 1-800-273-TALK (18002738255).
